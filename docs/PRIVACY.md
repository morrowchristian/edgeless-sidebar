# Privacy Policy for Edgeless Sidebar

**Last Updated:** June 15, 2024

## Overview

Edgeless Sidebar respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

- **Local Data**: Notes and pinned apps are stored locally in your Chrome browser storage
- **No External Data**: We do not collect, send, or store any data externally

### What We Don't Collect

- ❌ Personal information
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Login credentials
- ❌ Cookies
- ❌ Analytics data
- ❌ Usage statistics

## Data Storage

### Chrome Storage API

- All data (notes, pinned apps, settings) is stored using Chrome's built-in storage
- Data stays on your device
- No cloud sync (unless you choose to enable it in the future)

### Local Files

- No files are created or modified on your system
- No access to your file system

## Data Usage

### How Your Data Is Used

1. **Notes**: Displayed in the notes widget
2. **Pinned Apps**: Displayed in the app tower
3. **Settings**: Used for sidebar alignment and preferences

### Data Sharing

- **We Do Not Share Your Data**: Your data never leaves your browser
- **No Third-Party Access**: We don't use third-party services
- **No Analytics**: We don't track how you use the extension

## Permissions

### Why We Request Permissions

#### `activeTab`

- To detect the current tab for pinning functionality
- To get the URL and title of the active tab

#### `storage`

- To save your notes, pinned apps, and settings
- All data stays on your device

#### `scripting`

- To inject the sidebar into web pages
- Required for the sidebar to work

#### `declarativeNetRequest`

- To bypass iframe blocking on websites
- Allows you to view any website in the sidebar

#### `tabs`

- To detect tab changes for context awareness
- To get active tab information

### Permission Control

- You can revoke permissions at any time
- The extension will continue to work but features may be limited

## Data Deletion

### How to Delete Your Data

1. **Manually**:
   - Open Chrome Extensions (`chrome://extensions/`)
   - Click "Details" on Edgeless Sidebar
   - Click "Clear data" in the Storage section

2. **Uninstall**:
   - Uninstalling the extension removes all data
   - No data remains on your system

3. **Developer Tools**:
   - Open DevTools (F12)
   - Go to Application > Storage > Local Storage
   - Clear the extension's storage

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
