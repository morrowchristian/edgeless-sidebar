## Detailed Description

### 🎯 The Ultimate Productivity Sidebar

**Edgeless Sidebar** brings back the beloved sidebar functionality that Microsoft removed from Edge. Transform your Chrome browser into a powerful split-screen workspace where your favorite web apps live side-by-side with your content.

### ✨ Key Features

** 📱 App Tower**

- Pin your favorite web apps (Notion, YouTube, WhatsApp, etc.)
- Quick access with visual icons
- One-click to switch between apps
- Instantly pin any active tab

** 📝 Persistent Notes**

- Auto-saves every keystroke
- Never lose your thoughts
- Perfect for meeting notes or quick ideas
- Works offline

** 🧮 Calculator Widget**

- Built-in calculator in your sidebar
- Handle quick calculations without switching apps
- Keyboard support for efficiency

** 🔀 Context Awareness**

- Automatically switches content based on active tab
- Smart domain detection
- Seamless workflow integration

** ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts**

- `Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + E` to toggle alignment
- Click extension icon to show/hide sidebar
- Fast and efficient workflow
